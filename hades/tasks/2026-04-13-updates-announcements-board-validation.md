# Task Template

## Goal

Validate whether adding an SEO-friendly updates/announcements board that accumulates detailed product update posts is the right next product move for WZD, without implementing anything.

## User-visible outcome

- Team members can read a clear validation memo that explains whether the updates/announcements board solves a real problem for WZD now.
- The memo gives a bounded MVP recommendation, explicit non-goals, and measurable success criteria before any product or engineering work starts.

## In scope

- `UPDATES_VALIDATION_REPORT.md`
- Product validation against the current WZD positioning as a personalized bookmark + RSS first page
- A gstack-style evaluation covering real problem, timing, first users, risks, MVP scope, delays, and success validation

## Out of scope

- Any route, UI, schema, CMS, or SEO implementation
- Any change to landing copy, board behavior, or public pages
- Build, QA, benchmark, or security verification for code

## Risks

- The proposal can look strategically attractive while still diluting WZD's current product wedge.
- SEO upside can be overstated if the board depends on a publishing cadence the team cannot sustain.
- A broad validation memo can turn into implementation planning if scope is not kept tight.

## Pass criteria

- `UPDATES_VALIDATION_REPORT.md` exists at the repo root.
- The report is written in Korean.
- The report explicitly answers:
  - what real problem the idea solves
  - why it may or may not matter now
  - who it helps first
  - risks
  - MVP scope
  - what to delay
  - how to validate success
- The recommendation is grounded in WZD's current positioning and does not drift into implementation details.

## Required checks

- Confirm the report exists and covers all requested evaluation points.

## Extra checks if applicable

- None for this document-only validation task.
